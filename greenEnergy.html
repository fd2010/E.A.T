Optimize Your Energy Consumption

Energy-Saving Recommendations

Lights have consumed the most energy this week: 50kWh. Consider using LED bulbs or reducing usage in unoccupied areas.

Air conditioning is the second highest consumer at 40kWh. Try increasing the thermostat by 1-2 degrees to save energy.

Using power strips can help eliminate phantom loads from devices like monitors and projectors.

Set Saving Goals

Reduce overall energy consumption by 10% this month.

Limit lighting usage to 40kWh per week by turning off unnecessary lights.

Keep A/C usage below 35kWh per week by optimizing temperature settings.

Renewable Energy Tips

Consider switching to solar panels to offset energy costs.

Use energy-efficient appliances with high Energy Star ratings.

Monitor your power usage regularly to stay within set goals.
