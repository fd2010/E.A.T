A button away from energy saving!

E.A.T stands for Energy Analysis Tool.

This is your personal tool designed to help you analyse your energy consumption and provide you with insights on how to reduce your energy consumption.


We at PearCare.Inc prioritise and support world sustainability. E.A.T will be our first step to changing the world for the better.


Did you know that the average household wastes up to 30% of its energy? With E.A.T, you can identify these inefficiencies and take action.

Our advanced algorithms analyse your usage patterns and provide personalized recommendations that can help reduce your carbon footprint and save on utility bills.

E.A.T seamlessly integrates with your smart home devices to provide real-time monitoring and automated energy-saving suggestions.


Join thousands of environmentally conscious users who have already reduced their energy consumption by an average of 22% using E.A.T!
